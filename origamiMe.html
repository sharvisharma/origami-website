Let's Get Started

  1. step1=Fold the paper in half.
  2. step2=Fold the paper in half again.
  3. step3=Open the top flap of paper over to the right.
  4. step4=Squash Fold this flap down.
  5. step5=Turn the paper over.
  6. step6=Open the top flap of paper over to the left and Squash Fold it down.
  7. step7=Fold and Unfold the sides and the top along the dotted lines. Youâ€™ll use these creases in the next step.
  8. step8=Open up the top layer of paper and fold it up using the crease from the previous step.
  9. step9=Fold in both sides along the existing creases making a Petal Fold.
  10. step10=Turn the paper over.
  11. step11=Repeat steps 7-9 on this side.
  12. step12=Fold both sides to the centre along the dotted lines. Then repeat on the other side.
  13. step13=Inside Reverse Fold up both sides of the paper just like in a paper crane.
  14. step14=Reverse Fold the back part down to make the back legs.
  15. step15=Fold the top flap of paper down and repeat on the other side to make the front legs.
  16. step16=Reverse Fold the tip down to make the head.
  17. step17=Fold the back of the leg inside the model along the dotted line. Repeat on the other side.
  18. step18=Inside Reverse Fold the tip of the head.
  19. step19=The completed camel

  2. step2=Fold and unfold both sides to the centre.
  3. step3=Fold all the corners down along the diagonal dotted lines.
  4. step4=Open up each corner and Squash Fold them flat. See the next step to see how each corner looks after the Squash Fold.

  6. step6= Fold the edges of the paper down along the dotted lines.
  7. step7=Turn the paper over.
  8. step8=Fold the 4 flaps of paper out along the dotted lines.
  9. step9=Fold the paper in half over to the back.
  10. step10=Inside Reverse Fold the paper up along the dotted line.
  11. step11=Outside Reverse Fold the other side of the model down along the dotted line to form the tail.
  12. step12=Make another Outside Reverse Fold on the end of the tail.
  13. step13=The complete origami chameleon
